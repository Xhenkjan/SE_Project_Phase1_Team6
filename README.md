Project Details

Project Title: [Library Improvement]

Problem Statement:

School library has limited books, School library can't be accessed remotely, Need for students to understand other, higher achieving students.

Proposed Solution:

All physical books will be available online and will also have extra books that aren't available physically, so digital only books. Will also include approved students notes from high GPA students.

Project Scope:

Aim: [Increase the overall usefulness of the library]
Objectives:
[Make a digital catalog for all physical books in the library and the extra digital books.]
[Remote access to the resources in the library for faculty, students and paying customers (potentially)]
[Accurate system for searching and filtering through the database]
[A properly structured platform for students to share high quality notes]
[Increase the engagement of the common student with academic resources of the University.]
Application Description:

An application in which all of the physical books are recorded, where you can filter and search by genre and other categories, and will also tell you where in the library the physical books are located. Also will be able to sort through notes based on what department, bachelor, what lecture they cover etc. The users include students, members of the faculty and potentially outsiders as well (paid).
